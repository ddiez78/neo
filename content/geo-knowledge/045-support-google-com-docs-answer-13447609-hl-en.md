You can draft and refine content in Google Docs using information directly from your files in Drive, Chat, Gmail, and the web.

*   **Edit with a prompt:** Make the introduction more concise and professional.
*   **Refine with quick actions:** Highlight text to **Rephrase**, **Shorten**, **Elaborate**,**Bulletize**,or**Summarize**the content. You can also update the content to **More formal**or **More casual**.
*   **Edit using context from another file:** You can use your own documents as a source to add data, evidence, and citations.
*   **Use the writing style of an existing file:** Gemini can edit your document’s style mirroring the writing style of existing documents.

## [](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13447609?hl=en)Write, edit, or refine text with Gemini

To edit with Gemini, click Gemini ![Image 1](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/nDHdodQqXK8ChLmL7XG9Nt9SPyPBK076K9hg). Then, enter a prompt. When you edit, you don’t need to select any text since Gemini can understand the context of your doc and make broad or specific updates based on your instructions.

1.   On your computer, open a document in [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/).
2.   To target a specific section, at the bottom bar, mention the section in the prompt. For example: “Add evidence to my Research section.” 
    *   Or, to refine the whole document, you can enter a prompt like “Suggest places where I can better defend my argument.”

3.   Optional: Add sources for Gemini to reference when you write. [Learn how to reference other files to create content](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13447609#reference_other_files).
4.   Gemini will show suggestions directly in your document. 
    *   To apply changes individually, click Accept suggestion ![Image 2: Checkmark](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/zNqbtDTlKi1v5zdEL6CvD0zZNKea-HJtOwODIQwDkOc5BlwuewDJOS_thhc3AJVNbapP=w36-h36).
    *   To apply all changes, click **Accept all**.
    *   To reject all changes, click **Reject all**.

[Learn how to find & manage your Gemini in Workspace conversation history](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/16880047).

1.   On your computer, open a document in [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/).
2.   Select the text you want to rewrite.

    *   To refine the content, use the suggested text after you click: 

4.   Review the suggested changes. 
    *   To apply changes individually, click Accept suggestion ![Image 4: Checkmark](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/zNqbtDTlKi1v5zdEL6CvD0zZNKea-HJtOwODIQwDkOc5BlwuewDJOS_thhc3AJVNbapP=w36-h36).

## [](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13447609?hl=en)Reference other files to create content

You can ask Gemini to use specific files to write, edit, or refine content. For example: “Update the project timeline based on the latest updates from our meeting notes doc.”

1.   At the bottom bar, click Sources ![Image 5](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/nFiJUlnD533kXvNsQ79alqMYu5bUwgqgGvhl)![Image 6: and then](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/3_l97rr0GvhSP2XV5OoCkV2ZDTIisAOczrSdzNCBxhIKWrjXjHucxNwocghoUa39gw=h36)**Add from Drive** or other location. 
    *   Or, you can type “@” and then search and select from the menu.

2.   Select one or more files.
3.   Click **Add**.

### Select where Gemini can search for information

You can select the locations where Gemini can proactively search for content related to your prompt. [Learn how to use sources with Google Workspace with Gemini](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/16813283).

## Minimize or hide the bottom bar

By default, the Gemini bar automatically minimizes to clear your screen and help you focus on what you write.

To show the Gemini bar again, at the bottom, hover over Gemini ![Image 7](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/nDHdodQqXK8ChLmL7XG9Nt9SPyPBK076K9hg).

## Move bottom bar to side panel

To keep Gemini open while you work, you can move the bottom bar to the side panel. It provides a larger response display for improved readability.

1.   On your computer, open a document in [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/).
2.   At the bottom bar, enter your prompt. For example: 
    *   “Generate FAQs based on recent email conversations”
    *   “Add a table listing actions items and next steps”
    *   “Generate an email summarizing this document so that I can share it with executive shareholders via email”
    *   “Change all of the heading to blue color”

3.   Click Send action ![Image 8](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/2tCRcR8LOSG7tWpscao8mOoBPEoLVVkJzuku).
4.   At the bottom bar, click Switch to side panel ![Image 9](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/4LRJBWqVtGD7pI1HxmWIjAZQaEkv5tQrUr96).
5.   Optional: To switch back, at the top of the side panel, click Switch to bottom bar ![Image 10](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/ZHfxTEsJRoqKPTmJ1c1T9n2ZqjTpGMGsVZGO).

## Give feedback on generated text

Google Workspace with Gemini is constantly learning and may not be able to support your request.

If you get a suggestion that’s inaccurate or that you feel is unsafe, you can let us know by submitting feedback. Your feedback can help improve AI-assisted Workspace features and broader Google efforts in AI.

1.   In the bottom bar, click Leave feedback ![Image 11](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/SIcUfx3gSFQcykGeF8Iav2Iy2izeDkLFxVbH). 
2.   Select the data you want to share. This includes prompts and outputs.
3.   Enter additional feedback.
4.   Click**Submit**.

To provide general feedback on this feature, at the top, go to **Help**![Image 12: and then](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/3_l97rr0GvhSP2XV5OoCkV2ZDTIisAOczrSdzNCBxhIKWrjXjHucxNwocghoUa39gw=h36)**Help Docs improve**.

To report a legal issue, [learn how to report content for legal reasons](https://support.google.com/legal/answer/3110420).
