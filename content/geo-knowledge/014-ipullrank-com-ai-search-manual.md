## Search Has Changed. Here’s the Playbook.

![Image 2](https://ipullrank.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Group-2673.svg)

The AI Search Manual explains how search is changing and what to do about it. It covers the rise of conversational search platforms, the mechanics behind Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), and how Relevance Engineering helps you stay visible when traditional SEO falls short. If you want to understand where AI Search is headed and how to show up in it, this guide lays it all out.
