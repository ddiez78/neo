## Search Off the Record Podcast

Search Off the Record is a podcast show that takes you behind the scenes of Google Search and its inner workings. In each episode of the SOTR podcast, the folks from the Search Relations team will give you background info on the decision-making behind launches, take a deep dive into projects that Google Search teams are working on, and discuss currently trending topics in the SEO community at large. Have a listen!

[Subscribe to the podcast](https://pod.link/1512522198)
