In Geotelecom, part of our SEO strategy is focused on positioning your brand in the main artificial intelligence search engines. We analyze user queries, optimize content and define strategies so that your brand is positioned in the main search engines. **engines recognize and cite you**. In this way, we transform visibility into traffic, leads and real sales. This work is complemented by our [artificial intelligence services](https://www.geotelecom.es/en/artificial-intelligence/) to further enhance results.

## AI platforms 

key to your business

### ChatGPT - OpenAI

01/

One of the most consulted AI engines globally, used by millions of people to obtain information, recommendations and quick solutions. Positioning your brand on ChatGPT allows you to appear in relevant generative responses, increasing your visibility to potential customers who are actively searching for your products or services. In addition, you increase your authority and appear as a trusted reference in contexts where users make purchasing decisions based on AI-generated information.

![Image 15](https://www.geotelecom.es/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/rayo.png)

### Gemini - Google AI

02/

Google's AI integrated into its search and response generation ecosystem. Appearing in Gemini allows your brand to be cited in contextualized results, reinforcing your credibility with users. In addition, it improves the perception of authority and trust in your business, ensuring that your brand is considered as a relevant option when customers search for information, products or services similar to yours.

### Manus - Advanced AI

03/

This is a platform specialized in complex queries and generative analysis, used by professionals and companies looking for precise solutions. Positioning in Manus ensures your brand recognition in highly relevant contexts, connecting with segmented and potentially high-value audiences. This helps to consolidate the reputation of your business, generating trust in users who use generative answers to make informed decisions.
