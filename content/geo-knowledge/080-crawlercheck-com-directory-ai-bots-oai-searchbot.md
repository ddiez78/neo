## What is OAI-SearchBot?

OpenAI's prototype crawler for search features (SearchGPT). Unlike GPTBot (which trains models), this bot is used to fetch real-time information for user queries and provide citations.

## What happens if I block OAI-SearchBot?

### Low Impact

Blocking OAI-SearchBot will only prevent AI training on your content. This will NOT affect your search engine rankings or website traffic. Safe to block if you want to opt-out of AI training.
