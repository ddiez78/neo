## What Generative Engine Optimization Agencies Actually Do

**A generative engine optimization agency** helps brands improve how their content is retrieved, summarized, and cited inside AI-generated answers across systems such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity.

Generative engine optimization agencies are sometimes also called **AI search visibility agencies**, **answer engine optimization (AEO) agencies**, **LLM visibility consultants**, or **generative engine optimization companies**.

Some buyers also search for **generative engine optimization companies**, **service providers**, or **GEO vendors**. In practice, these terms usually refer to agencies or specialized teams that help brands improve AI visibility, citation likelihood, and cross-engine presence.

Unlike traditional SEO agencies that prioritize rankings and traffic, GEO agencies focus on improving visibility inside AI systems such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity.

Some [AI SEO agencies](https://wellows.com/blog/top-ai-seo-agencies/) still prioritize rankings, whereas GEO agencies focus on how brands are selected and cited inside AI-generated answers.

Some **specialist agencies** operate at the intersection of **answer engine optimization (AEO)** and **generative engine optimization (GEO)**, particularly for **B2B** companies where AI-generated answers influence evaluation and buying decisions.

Their primary role is to align brand information with how AI systems understand, retrieve, and reuse knowledge through entities and relationships rather than standalone keywords. This is why [entity-based content](https://wellows.com/blog/entity-based-content/) is foundational to AI search visibility.

To support AI retrieval, agencies apply operational practices that mirror how large language models process structured context.

While agencies handle strategy and execution, many teams also use generative engine optimization tools or AI visibility platforms to track citations across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.

These practices depend on semantic clarity, internal consistency, and machine-readable structure, making [structured data](https://wellows.com/blog/structured-data/) a core requirement for sustained inclusion in AI-generated responses.

**AI answer optimization prioritizes selection, not ranking:** Visibility depends on whether content is chosen as a cited source inside generated responses.

**Entity strength influences citation likelihood:** Brands with clear, consistent entities are easier for AI systems to recognize and reference reliably.

**LLM-friendly structure enables reuse:** Well-structured pages are more easily chunked, summarized, and reused across multiple AI answers.

**AI visibility replaces rank tracking:** Monitoring citations and presence across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity provides a more accurate view of discovery.

## The 7 Criteria Used to Evaluate GEO Agencies
