# Entity Optimization

## Goal
Improve how LLMs understand the brand as a clear entity in its category.

## Signals
- Brand name appears consistently across official pages.
- Website explains the category, product, market, and use cases clearly.
- Product names and brand aliases are consistent.
- Third-party sources connect the brand to the same category.

## Recommendation Rules
- If the brand appears in fewer runs than competitors, improve entity clarity.
- If official sources are not cited, strengthen official pages and brand schema.
- If company profile fields are incomplete, fill brand description, markets, keywords, and official URLs.

## Output Examples
- Rewrite the company profile with stronger category language.
- Add product/category pages that describe what the brand does.
- Create comparison pages that associate the brand with relevant alternatives.
