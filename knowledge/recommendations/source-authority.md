# Source Authority

## Goal
Increase the probability that LLMs cite credible sources that mention the brand.

## Signals
- Repeated source domains in prompt runs.
- Competitors appearing in more authoritative domains.
- Lack of official or third-party sources that mention the brand.

## Recommendation Rules
- If source count is low, prioritize authoritative third-party mentions.
- If competitors dominate sources, identify domains where the brand is absent.
- If official URLs are missing, add them to the company profile.

## Output Examples
- Pitch expert quotes to industry blogs.
- Publish data-driven resources worth citing.
- Build pages that answer comparison and category-intent queries.
